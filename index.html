Our Vision

CellularLayer is dedicated to creating Minimum Viable Prototypes (MVPs) of a **biological hypervisor**. By genetically engineering simplified organisms, we aim to establish a host platform capable of running isolated "guest" biological processes. Our goal is to develop an accessible framework for creating and testing hypotheses about gene function and protein interactions in a controlled, live cellular environment.

Our initial MVP explorations will focus on: Virtualizing basic genetic scenarios within a minimal, synthetic organism to observe their isolated impact on fundamental cellular processes without interfering with the host's core functions.
